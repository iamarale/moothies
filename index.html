Blueberry Apple

Prep Time

5 mins

Serving

4

Calories

200

Protein

2g

Sugar

34g

Ingredients

  • 4 cups frozen blueberries

  • 4 apples

  • 2 cups of almond milk

Instructions

  1. Dice apples into blended sizes, put all the blueberries and apples into blender

  2. Add almond milk and blend until it has a smooth consistency and is drinkable

  3. Serve in glass and enjoy!
